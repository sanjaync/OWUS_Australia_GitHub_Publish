\section{OzFlux Tower Network: Detailed Site
Reports}\label{ozflux-tower-network-detailed-site-reports}

\subsection{Introduction}\label{introduction}

OzFlux is a national network of flux towers that provides continuous
measurements of carbon, water, and energy exchanges between terrestrial
ecosystems and the atmosphere across Australia and New Zealand. This
report provides a detailed overview of the tower sites, including their
physical configurations and key metadata.

Total sites documented: 46
